Gallery:

The left image shows the face-shapes detected by the algorithms and surrounds the face-shapes with a rectangle. The right side image is simply the application of a color mask on top of those detected face-shapes. The mask gives more definition to the nuances of wach face-shape. but many face-shapes are hard to discern still. Zooming in can help a bit, but imagination helps the most! Sometimes we have words for what we see, sometimes we don't. Sometimes the face-shape is clear, sometimes it isn't. When there are a large amount of faces, the image seems very chaotic. But it still may be interesting! Please do not take these face-shapes seriously. They really are because of the effects of wind and lighting, etc.. Enjoy!

Gallery:

The images on the right shows the face-shapes detected by the algorithms and surrounds the face-shapes with a rectangle. The left side image is simply the application of a color mask on top of those detected face-shapes. The mask gives more definition to the nuances of each face-shape, but many shapes are still hard to discern. Zooming in can help a bit, but imagination helps the most!!!! Though some images look overwhelming when there are a large amount of face-shapes, it may stil be interesting to zoom in and see what you can pick out! Please do not take these face-shapes seriously. They really are because of the effects of wind and lighting, etc.. Enjoy!
